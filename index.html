What is “The Cloud”?

Everyone just loooves this term, don’t they? The Cloud, in tech speak, refers to storing and accessing your applications on the Internet instead of your computer’s network. Paragon, our newest ERP solution, is a cloud application, which means you can access it from anywhere in the world, at any time of the day, as long as you’re connected to the Internet.

Internships
We use our experience to shape yours

At Jonar, interns don’t just fetch coffee or make photocopies. Well, sometimes they do, but only for themselves. Our interns work side-by-side with the Jonar team on projects that have a real and meaningful impact. We believe that interns are brilliant contributors, out of the box thinkers and valued collaborators for our team. We love our interns because they bring a new and fresh perspective to everything we do. And, sometimes, we love them so much that we hire them straight out of their internships.

Social Responsibility
Doing good in all that we do

We run our business with a genuine dedication to our 7 core values. We take these seriously seriously. But, our business is more than just a business. We are truly committed to doing good in everything we do. As a result, we provide a system that is not only a great product, but that also helps great companies. We also work with great people who are also committed to the overall greater good and we support worthwhile organizations that work hard every day to make a difference. We know this sounds a little Miss America, but if we can make the world even the teeniest bit better, then we will strive to do so.
